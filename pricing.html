Commission Process

  • Initial Consultation: Discuss the vision, size, and details of the portrait.
  • Photography Session: Capture reference photos for the artwork.
  • Initial Sketch: Review and approve the preliminary sketch.
  • Underpainting: Develop the verdaccio layer for tonal accuracy.
  • Final Painting: Complete the portrait with fine details and impasto techniques.

Payment Terms

Payments are divided into three stages:

  1. Initial Sketch Approval: 1/3 of the total price.
  2. Underpainting Completed: 1/3 of the total price.
  3. Finished Artwork: Final 1/3 of the price.

Crating, shipping, and insurance costs are borne by the buyer. Turnaround times vary from
several weeks to months, depending on the complexity and size of the commission.
